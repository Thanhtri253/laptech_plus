At TechNova, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what personal information we collect and how we use it.

1. What information we collect

  • Personal info: Your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number when you place an order.

  • Payment info: Your payment details are processed securely by our payment partners (like Stripe, PayPal, etc.). We do not store your full credit card number.

  • Cookies & usage data: We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your cart, and analyze website traffic to improve your experience.

2. How we use your information

We use your info for three main reasons:

  • To fulfill your order: To process your payment, ship your products, and send you order confirmations.

  • To provide support: To answer your questions and help with any issues.

  • For marketing (with your consent): To send you special offers or new product news, but only if you opt-in to our newsletter.

3. How we share your information

We never sell your personal data. We only share it with essential partners to run our business:

  • Payment processors (to handle your payment).

  • Shipping companies (to deliver your order).

4. How we protect your information

We use industry-standard security measures, like SSL encryption, to protect your data during transmission. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized employees only.

5. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
